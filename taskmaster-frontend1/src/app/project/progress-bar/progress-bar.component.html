🌱 Environmental Events

🌍 Earth Day

A day to promote environmental awareness and protection on April 22nd each year.

♻️ World Recycling Day

Celebrated on March 18th, focusing on the importance of recycling and reducing waste.

🌳 Arbor Day

A day dedicated to planting trees, celebrated on the last Friday in April.

🌊 World Oceans Day

Celebrated on June 8th, aiming to raise awareness about the importance of oceans.

🌿 International Day for Biological Diversity

A day to increase understanding and awareness of biodiversity issues on May 22nd.
